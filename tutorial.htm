Controls

The controls are simple, consisting of only mouse movements and clicking! Each microgame will give an objective you have to complete. If you fail to complete this within the allotted time, you'll lose a life. As you complete more games, the amount of time given will slowly decrease, pushing your reaction speed further and further! Try to complete as many games as you can!
